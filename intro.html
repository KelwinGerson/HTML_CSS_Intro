You are a cabbage or a rose ??

There is the story of a rose and a cabbage. The first one is born closed and during its life it opens. Cabbage is born open and during life it closes. It has to do with our lives. Sometimes we learn something and then it doesn't evolve anymore, this is compared to cabbage, which is born open and then closes.
